Our Story

Crafted with Passion,
Delivered with Pride

Welcome to A.S Cube Liquor Hub — a premier destination for fine spirits nestled in the heart of Sirjananagar, Bhaktapur. We believe every great occasion deserves an exceptional drink.

From the smoky richness of Jack Daniel's Tennessee Whiskey to the smooth elegance of our premium label selections, we bring you a handpicked collection of the finest domestic and imported spirits available in Nepal.

Whether you're a seasoned connoisseur or just beginning your journey into the world of fine liquors, our knowledgeable team is here to guide you to your perfect bottle.
